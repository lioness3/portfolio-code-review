About Me

Hello, my name is Joann Carter. I prefer to go by Jo or Jojo. I consider myself an adventurous person because I thrive on being spontaneous. For example, I recently moved to the West Coast.

Besides traveling and random summer adventures, I lived in Southern NH my entire life. Being spontataneous has its advantages because you aquire many life experiances by trying new things and learning others perspectives on life. I have tried a wide variety of career paths such as:

  • Security Sales Manager
  • Personal Shopper
  • Automotive Repair Estimator
  • Mixologist
  • Mancurist

I was a manicurist for most of my working career. I worked at a salon in NH for 9 years. It was a challenge trying to prove my income with commission based jobs. After a year of house hunting, I decided to find a studio apartemnt to rent until the housing market crashed.

On memorial day of 2019, my cousin from the West Coast came to visit. Hearing my frustration, he suggested a coding bootcamp as a fast track career to stabilitize my income. Even though he was pretty convincing, I declined due to the lease I would be signing the very next day.Turns out, the landlord chose a another tenant without pets. I took the rejection as a sign to go live near my cousin and attend a coding bootcap, Epicodus.
