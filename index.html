About Me

Hello, my name is Joann Carter. I prefer to go by Jo or Jojo. I consider myself an adventurous person because I thrive on being spontaneous. For example, I recently moved to the West Coast.

Besides traveling and random summer adventures, I lived in Southern NH my entire life. Being spontataneous has its advantages because you aquire many life experiances by trying new things and learning others perspectives on life. I have tried a wide variety of career paths such as:

  • Security Sales Manager
  • Personal Shopper
  • Automotive Repair Estimator
  • Mixologist
  • Mancurist

As you can see, I have tried a wide variety of jobs. My most long lived career was a manicurist at a fancy salon in NH. I was there for 9 years. It was difficult trying to prove my income with commission based jobs. After a year of house hunting, i decided to find a studio apartemnt to rent untill the housing market crashed.

On memorial day of 2019 my cousin from the west coast came to visit. Hearing my frustration, he suggested a coding bootcamp for a fast track career to stabilitize my income. Even though he was pretty convincing, I declined because I was signing a leasing for a studio the very next day.Turns out the landlord chose a tenant with no pets so i took that as a sign to go live near my cousin and attend Epicodus.
